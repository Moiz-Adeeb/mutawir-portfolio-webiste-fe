About Us

About Company:

Mutawir is a student-founded digital services company established on 17 January 2026 by two friends who are pursuing a BS in Computer Science. What began as a freelance journey has now grown into a dedicated company with a clear vision: to provide reliable, modern, and creative digital solutions for businesses and individuals.

Before officially launching Mutawir, we worked as freelancers and gained hands-on experience by delivering quality projects to different clients. This practical experience helped us understand real client needs, industry standards, and the importance of clean design and efficient development. Today, we bring that experience into every project we handle as a company.

At Mutawir, we offer a wide range of services including website development, application development, UI/UX design, graphic design, branding, and logo design. We focus on creating user-friendly, visually appealing, and performance-driven digital products that help brands grow and stand out in the digital world.

As computer science students, we combine technical knowledge, creativity, and modern tools to deliver solutions that are both functional and innovative. Our goal is to build long-term relationships with our clients by providing honest communication, quality work, and on-time delivery.
